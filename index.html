A Note For

My Love Nancy

♥

You know, life has a funny way of surprising us. That day, I wasn’t looking for anything special….just another random chat on Walkie Talkie, just another voice in the crowd. But then you came along. December 17th. It might’ve been just another ordinary day to the world, but for us ? That was the day everything changed.

Out of millions of people, across all that noise and distance, somehow, your voice found mine. And from that very first conversation, something just clicked. It felt like I had been waiting for something, even though I didn’t know it at the time.

Nancy, you are the most wonderful person I’ve ever known, and I don’t say that lightly. It’s not just the sound of your voice or the way you laugh. It’s the kindness you carry, the way you make people feel truly seen. The way you made me feel , even from kilometres away and through a screen.

I never believed in love at first convo, until I met you. You’ve shown me a kind of happiness I never thought was possible. Being with you feels like coming home. You’re my best friend, and the love of my life.

I can’t wait to create more memories with you, to support each other’s dreams, and to grow together. You make me want to be a better person, and I promise to always love you, cherish you, and stand by your side through everything.

I love you more than I could ever put into words. More than any distance could measure. You’re my unexpected blessing, my beautiful accident.

I want to be the reason behind your smile, the one you can always count on, and the one who will love you unconditionally.

Thank you for being you, Nancy. Thank you for choosing me. I love you more than words can say.

Forever & Always

With All My Love

P.S. — December 17th will always be my favorite day of the year. Because that's the day I found you
